How to resolve your problem with sending a request?

In top right of your screen you have an icon FAQ (for Frequently Asked Questions). You have then access to the various questions and solutions put online by the person in charge of data processing.

How to announce a technical problem?

1) Select the emergency level of your problem (bu default the selected urgency is "Medium").

2) Indicate the number of your computer.

If you do not know the number of the concerned item you must click on the icon "?" :

  • A form will open and you will have to indicate the first letters of your name, the name of the concerned item or its serial number before clicking on "Search".
  • Then a list should appear, you will find the searched number in the list. It is enough for you to click on the line concerned to update the form.
  • If your name does not appear contact the person in charge of data processing.

For an intervention of the general type no number is necessary

3) Describe your problem

Be most precise as possible in your description. Then, the treatment will be only faster.

4) Follow-up by mail

It is possible that two other fields are in the form:

  • The field « inform me of the taken actions pursuant ». If you answer yes in this field, you will receive a notification by e-mail, each time that a technician will add a message of follow-up to your intervention.
  • The field « my E-mail » which will enable you to specify an address to receive these follow-ups.

Once this form correctly filled click on "Submit message".

You can also add a new request for control while clicking on the icon "Add a new intervention" on the top right of your screen.

How to follow the requests you already sent ?

It is also possible for you to follow online the evolution of our open or finished interventions For that click on the "Followup up the interventions" icon on the top right.

The list of all your interventions in progress or finished should appear. To obtain more information concerning each intervention, you can click on the «  informations   » link

How to reserve a material?

It is possible for you to hold materials. For that click on the icon "Reservations" in top on the right of your screen.

If materials are available in loan, you will see the list of it. You can click on that which interests you. Then, you will see the calendar of reservation. It is enough for you then to click on an icon "Book" and to specify the parameters of your reservation: beginning, ending, periodicity and comment.

How to modify your personalized parameters for this application?

The language in which you wish to see the interface, if you want to use another language select «  your language   » then click on «  Change   ».

To modify your password, use the password form on the top.

How to logout?

If you want to logout from the GLPI application, click on the «  Lougout   » button on the top right.
